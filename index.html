Thesis Book

My thesis book comprehensively summarizes my research journey and findings. It begins with a literature review of different types of transfer carts and their applications. Next, I used functional analysis tools to identify key characteristics and select appropriate solutions based on predefined criteria. After identifying a solution, I developed it through 3D modeling using CAD software, adapting it for desired kinematics and sizing. I then conducted FEA simulations to verify structural integrity and applied the same process to the hydraulic system. Finally, I designed and simulated the electric circuits to power and control the cart, utilizing solar energy to optimize auxiliary circuit power consumption.
